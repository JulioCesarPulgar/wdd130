Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our website is to provide a central hub for our customers to access important information about our products and services. We aim to make it easy for users to navigate our site and find the information they need quickly and efficiently. Additionally, our website serves as a platform for customers to make purchases, track orders, and connect with our customer support team. Our goal is to create a seamless online experience that enhances the customer journey and helps us build strong relationships with our audience.

Audience

Our target audience for this website are outdoor enthusiasts who are interested in water rafting. They are aged between 18 to 45 and come from various financial backgrounds. Our audience seeks adventure and enjoys spending time in nature. They expect to find information on our water rafting packages, pricing, safety measures, and experience level required for each package. Our website content is optimized for mobile devices, allowing our audience to easily access the information they need while on the go. By targeting our content to our specific audience, we can create a website that meets their needs and enhances their experience.
